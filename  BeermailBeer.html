Volfas Engelman, the legendary beer of Lithuania of the interwar period, has returned and is as great now as it was then. The brand is named after its founders –Raphail Wolf and Ferdinand Engelmann. Volfas Engelman was the largest brewery in interwar Kaunas and it brewed the most popular beer in Lithuania at the time. Creating a new sophisticated image, we have created a reserved, yet stylish brand for a gentleman. Volfas Engelman is the fastest growing premium beer brand in Lithuania. Currently, we are offering for export a range of Volfas Engelman beers: pilsner Pilzeno, lager Rinktinis, wheat beer Balta and dark beer Bohemijos Tamsusis.

MANGO MILKSHAKE PALE ALE 
ABV: 5.6%

Completely unpredictable and exclusive light type ale, moderately bitter, very refreshing, fragrant with mangoes and tropics. Bright subtle yellow color, that reminiscents of mango juice. It’s an unforgettable ale that shows how standard beers differ from craft.

HOPPY LAGER
ABV: 5.5%

Unlike many traditional lager type beers, Hoppy Lager is much more expressive, more intense dark amber–chestnut color, it has unique aroma of American hops, and a full, memorable flavor.

NEW ENGLAND INDIA PALE ALE
ABV: 5.0%

Brewing of this type beer began only in 2011 in the United States. Our NEIPA is in no way inferior in its taste and aroma to the most famous and well-known NEIPA beers in the world. It is super fresh, fruity and quenches thirst with an exclusive aroma of citrus, tropical fruits, pineapple, lychee, melon and apricot

RASPBERRY PORTER
BEER MAIL

Dark and viscous beer texture is created by using caramel, roasted barley malt, hulled oats. The moderately noticeable raspberry makes it a dessert type beer, therefore exclusive among the other porter beers.

BOHEMIJOS TAMSUSIS
ABV: 4.2%

Velvety dark, soft due to that especially suitable for summer evenings “BOHEMIJOS Tamsusis” beer has complemented the Volfas Engelman family.

VOLFAS ENGELMAN BALTIC PORTER
ABV: 6%

Baltic Porter is a beer type that is known as the only bottom-fermented porter in the world. It is a dark copper-brown beer with a thick head and a slightly sweet roasted caramel touch to the rich aroma.

VOLFAS ENGELMAN LAGER “RINKTINIS”
ABV: 5.2%

Bottom-fermented lager. The aromatic Perle and Magnum hops, together with the unique brewer’s yeast reveal a rich taste.

VOLFAS ENGELMAN PILSNER “PILZENO”
ABV: 4.7%

Bottom-fermented pilsner. Lithuanian light malt and the abundance of hops give the taste its richness. Czech and various German hops are used.
